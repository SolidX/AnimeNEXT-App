About the App

For [REDACTED] reasons we don't have schedule in the Guidebook app this year. After discussing it with a fellow staff member, I decided to build this janky PWA as a proof of concept. After a few hours of work over the weekend, I had something somewhat useful. Since you're reading this, things must have escalated from there.

For inquiries, feedback, or to sign up to futher develop this app you can contact greg@animenext.org after the convention.

Big thanks to all who pitched in at the last second to make this happen in time for con! Esepcially Chibi & Liz!
